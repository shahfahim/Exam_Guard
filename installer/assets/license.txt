PRIVACY NOTICE
--------------
ExamGuard collects the following data during an active exam session ONLY:

  - Active window titles (to detect task-switching)
  - Clipboard contents (to detect copy-paste of answers)
  - Screenshots (taken at configurable intervals)
  - Keystroke counts (totals only, not individual key content)
  - USB device insertion events

All collected data is stored LOCALLY on this machine only.
No data is transmitted to any remote server, cloud service, or third party.
Data is stored in: %APPDATA%\ExamGuard\

By installing and using ExamGuard, you acknowledge and agree to this
data collection during exam sessions on this device.
